About

A small agency, by design.

We work with a focused roster across endurance and combat sports. Every athlete gets direct access to a senior agent — not a junior assistant — and a plan tailored to where they actually are in their career, not where a template says they should be.

Our team has backgrounds in competitive sport, sponsorship sales, and event operations. That mix shapes how we negotiate, who we introduce you to, and how we protect your time during a season.
